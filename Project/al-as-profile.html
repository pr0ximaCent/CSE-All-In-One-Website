Hi, this is Assaduzzaman, an alumnus of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology.I received the B. Sc. Engineering degree in electrical and electronics engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET), Bangladesh, in 2001. From 2001 to onwards have been serving as a faculty member in the same University.I received PhD degree from University of Ulsan, Korea in 2010. Currently, I am a Professor in Computer Science and Engineering at Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology, Chittagong, Bangladesh. Currently,I am in charge of Head of the same Department since April 23, 2019.

I have more than fifty publications in referred journals and conference proceedings. I serve as a TPC member in different international conferences and a reviewer of several international journals and conferences. Recently, I have been selected as an evaluator of the Call for Proposals of TEIN*CC and Asi@Connect project.

I feel myself lucky to be an alumnus of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology. Currently, I have been serving the CUET Alumni Community as a member of CUET Alumni Association (CUAA).Throughout my career, I have made notable contributions through various publications. These include the publication titled "Impact Analysis of Facebook in Family Bonding" in 2016 and "A Statistical Framework for Detecting Diabetes Types" in 2015.
