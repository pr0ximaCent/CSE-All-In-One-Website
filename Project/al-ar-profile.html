Hi, this is Shamshul Arefin, an alumnus of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology. I completed my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Engineering in Khulna University (KU) and then completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), Bangladesh.Hold a Doctor of Engineering degree from Hiroshima University, Japan.

In terms of my professional experience, I served as a Lecturer at CUET, Chittagong from June 30, 2003, to January 28, 2007. Following that, I held the position of Assistant Professor at CUET, Chittagong from January 29, 2007, to March 30, 2014. I then progressed to become an Associate Professor at CUET, Chittagong from March 31, 2014, to July 1, 2015. Currently, I hold the position of Professor at CUET, Chittagong, starting from July 2, 2015.

I feel myself lucky to be an alumnus of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology. Currently, I have been serving the CUET Alumni Community as a member of CUET Alumni Association (CUAA).Throughout my career, I have made notable contributions through various publications. These include the publication titled "Impact Analysis of Facebook in Family Bonding" in 2016 and "A Statistical Framework for Detecting Diabetes Types" in 2015.
